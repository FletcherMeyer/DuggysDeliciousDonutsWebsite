Welcome to Duggy's Delicious Donuts!

Founded in 1995 in the heart of Maplewood, Duggy's Delicious Donuts is a family-run bakery chain now delighting customers across five locations statewide. Led by owner Duggy Thompson and head baker Grandma Gertrude, we handcraft every donut with love using locally sourced ingredients and time-honored family recipes. From our signature Long John filled with creamy vanilla custard to seasonal specials like Pumpkin Spice, our donuts are made fresh daily to bring a smile to every bite.

Join us at our flagship Maplewood store or any of our locations to experience the magic of Duggy's. Check out our menu for our full range of classic and specialty donuts, see our hours to plan your visit, or meet our team—from IT wizard Darius Dwayne to aspiring manager Ethan Coulthard—who keep the donut dream alive!
